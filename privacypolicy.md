  <h1>Privacy Policy</h1>
  <p class="legal-meta">Effective: March 19, 2026<br>Last Updated: March 19, 2026</p>

  <p>JMG ("we", "us") respects your privacy and complies with the Hong Kong Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance.</p>

  <h2>1. Information we collect</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Name and email address when you register via Google, Facebook, or Apple login</li>
    <li>Chat messages and job details you post publicly on the platform</li>
    <li>Device and usage data (IP address, browser type) for security and service improvement</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>2. How we use your information</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Create and manage your account</li>
    <li>Connect BOSSES with jobbers</li>
    <li>Prevent fraud and abuse</li>
    <li>Communicate service updates</li>
    <li>Comply with legal obligations</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>3. Account registration via social login</h2>
  <p>When you create an account using Google, Facebook, or Apple login:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>We receive only your name and email address directly from the provider.</li>
    <li>We use this information solely to create and manage your JMG account, communicate with you, and prevent fraud.</li>
    <li>We do not request or store additional profile data, photos, or identifiers from these providers.</li>
  </ul>
  <p>Third-party privacy policies apply.</p>
  <p>The privacy practices of Google, Facebook, and Apple govern how they collect, use, and share your information when you use their login services. Please review their privacy policies:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><a href="https://policies.google.com/privacy">Google: https://policies.google.com/privacy</a></li>
    <li><a href="https://www.facebook.com/privacy/policy">Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/privacy/policy</a></li>
    <li><a href="https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/">Apple: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/</a></li>
  </ul>

  <h2>4. Your rights</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Access: Request the personal data we hold</li>
    <li>Correction: Update inaccurate name/email</li>
    <li>Deletion: Permanently delete your account and data</li>
    <li>Contact: <a href="mailto:privacy@jobmonkeygo.com">privacy@jobmonkeygo.com</a></li>
  </ul>

  <h2>5. Data retention</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Account data kept while your account is active</li>
    <li>Deleted within 30 days of account deletion request</li>
    <li>Chat/job history kept 90 days for dispute resolution, then anonymized</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>6. Security</h2>
  <p>We use industry-standard security including encryption, access controls, and monitoring. However, no online service is 100% secure.</p>

  <h2>7. Changes to this policy</h2>
  <p>We'll notify you of material changes via email or in-app notice.</p>
